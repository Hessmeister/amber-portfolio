About

Photo

Born in The Hague — the one by the sea that isn’t the capital — now based in Amsterdam.

Active in film production since 2002 and a full-time editor since 2007. Her work spans commercials, online films, music videos, short films, dance films, and documentaries — including work for Amazon Prime.

Known for a strong sense of rhythm and timing, she brings a musical quality to every edit. Fast, precise, and always searching for the emotional core of the story.

She maintains her own edit suite and a portable setup for location work. A big fan of poolside edit suites.
